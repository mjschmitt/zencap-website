Risk Factors and Investment Considerations

KEY INVESTMENT RISKS

Technology Risks
  • Technical Scalability: Challenges in scaling qubit counts and coherence
  • Error Correction: Quantum error correction still developing
  • Competition from Classical: Advances in classical computing reducing quantum advantage
  • Standards and Protocols: Lack of industry standardization
Market Risks
  • Adoption Timeline: Commercial applications may take longer than expected
  • Talent Shortage: Limited quantum computing expertise available
  • Capital Requirements: High R&D costs and uncertain returns
  • Regulatory Uncertainty: National security considerations affecting development

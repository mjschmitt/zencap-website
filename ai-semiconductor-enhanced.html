Supply Chain Vulnerabilities & Mitigation

Critical Bottlenecks
  • EUV Lithography: ASML monopoly on sub-7nm production

  • HBM Memory: SK Hynix/Samsung duopoly on high-bandwidth memory
  • Rare Earth Materials: China controls 70% of processing
Diversification Efforts
  • CHIPS Act Investment: $52B US semiconductor manufacturing
  • European Chips Act: €43B EU production capacity
  • Japan-Taiwan Alliance: Joint advanced packaging facilities

DISRUPTION Fintech Revolution: Reshaping Financial Services Landscape

The fintech disruption has evolved from payment innovation to comprehensive financial infrastructure transformation. Our analysis of 1,200+ fintech companies reveals accelerating market capture across lending, wealth management, insurance, and embedded finance, fundamentally altering competitive dynamics in the $26 trillion global financial services market.

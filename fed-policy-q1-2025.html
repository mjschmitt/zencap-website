CONCLUSION Investment Outlook & Portfolio Strategy

The Federal Reserve's March 2025 policy shift creates a multi-asset opportunity set favoring duration extension and quality credit exposure. Our base case anticipates 150-200bp of cumulative easing through year-end, supporting risk asset valuations while benefiting interest-sensitive sectors.

Portfolio construction should emphasize flexibility given the evolving macro backdrop. The combination of moderating inflation pressures and potential growth deceleration supports our tactical overweight in duration-sensitive assets while maintaining quality bias in credit selection.
